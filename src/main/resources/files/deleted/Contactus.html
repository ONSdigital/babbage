This is ONS Alpha website build.

We have done this to demonstrate what the ONS website could look like in the future. Throughout the process we have, and will continue to, test with real users of the website. This website will close on the 5 January 2015.

Content on this website is not conclusive and may not be up-to-date or correct. Please refer to the current ONS website for the most recent statistics.

Feedback

Comments on structure, presentation or usability of this prototype is greatly appreciated and will be used to inform future developments.

You can provide feedback by:

  • Sending an Email to xxxxxxxxxxx@ons.gsi.gov.uk
  • Or completing our survey
